ABOUT ME
My name is Eva Waldron and I am a Media Arts and Design student at James Madison University, set to graduate in May 2025. I'm from Richmond, VA where I was raised by my parents in the leafy jungle of Forest Hill, located south of the James River. Growing up a short car ride into the city's downtown area, I've always been attracted to and inspired by populated settings where endless possibilities of personal connections can intertwine.

This inspiration has led to a focus in online accessibility and navigation, especially when it comes to the healthcare industry and the intricacies of receiving care. My technical skills, accumulated through course work and personal projects, have allowed me insights into the digital world of innovation and creation for human good that I will carry into a professional environment.
